Our Story

Batticaloa Municipal Council

The Batticaloa Municipal Council (BMC) is the local authority for the city of Batticaloa in eastern Sri Lanka. Established as a Municipal Council in 1967, BMC is responsible for providing essential public services including roads, sanitation, housing, libraries, public parks, and recreational facilities.

The Batticaloa Tourism initiative was launched to showcase our city's extraordinary natural beauty — the lagoon, pristine beaches, Dutch heritage, and vibrant Tamil culture — to the world, while creating sustainable economic opportunities for local communities.
